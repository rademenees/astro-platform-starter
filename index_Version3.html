Marakolliya's Turtles

Marakolliya Beach is a sanctuary for the magnificent  turtle. These gentle creatures are known for their unique nesting behaviors.

During specific times of the year, these turtles grace our shores. We are dedicated to their conservation and offer respectful viewing opportunities.

Our night tours provide a rare chance to observe these turtles in their natural habitat. It's an unforgettable experience that supports local conservation efforts.

Peacock Turtle Night Tours offers visitors a real, natural, and responsible turtle-watching experience, guided by someone who truly knows this beach and its wildlife. This is not a show or a zoo – it is nature, exactly as it happens.

OUR TOUR
Peacock Turtle Night Tours is a locally owned guiding service based on Marakolliya Beach, Tangalle, Sri Lanka. The tours are led by Saman Kalinga, an experienced local guide who has been protecting and observing sea turtles on this beach for over 16 years

Every night during the nesting season, visitors are invited to join a guided walk along the beach to witness one of nature’s most remarkable events – wild sea turtles coming ashore to lay their eggs. Marakolliya Beach is home to five different species of sea turtles, making it one of the most important nesting areas in southern Sri Lanka. The main nesting season runs from December to May/June.

All tours are conducted with deep respect for wildlife and international conservation guidelines. Only red light is used, as white light can disturb and disorient nesting turtles. This ensures a safe, ethical and authentic wildlife experience without harming the animals.
